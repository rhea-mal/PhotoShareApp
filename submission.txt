STORY #1 Extend User Profile with Usage
Story points: 3
Brief description to see the story in action:
Beneath each user details page, see both thumbnail pictures. The first thumbnail shows the most recently uploaded photo with the time and date it was added. The second thumbnail shows the photo with the most comments. The maximum number of comments received is listed below.

STORY #7 Favorite lists of photos for users
Story points: 6
Brief description to see the story in action:
Beneath each user photo, a small heart is used to favorite photos as a button that becomes filled in when favorited. This adds a photo to a particular user's favorites list. To see a users favorites, the button in the top left of the top bar goes to a page where all the favorited images thumbnails appear. Clicking on each picture expands the photo to include a caption of the photo upload date along with a gray X at the bottom left to remove a photo from favorites.
